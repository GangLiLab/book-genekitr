üß¨ Welcome to genekitr!

The book is a tutorial for gene information query, gene identifier conversion, gene enrichment analysis, and publication-level plotting. If you are new to R, do not worry, I will set enough example codes to guide you through the book. After reading this book, you could plot figures based on your analysis result.

üç± Content

  • Part 1 (Search Information) introduces how to search gene metadata and PubMed papers.
  • Part 2 (ID Conversion) describes ID conversion among symbol & alias, NCBI Entrez, Ensembl, Uniprot and microarray probe.
  • Part 3 (Enrichment Analysis) describes the two widely used functional enrichment methods over-representation analysis (ORA) and functional class scoring (FCS).
  • Part 4 (Plotting) includes 14 GO plots, 7 KEGG plots, 5 GSEA plots, 2 Venn plots, and 1 Volcano plot.
